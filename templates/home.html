About

Me

I have suffered from migraines for around 25 years and I've been writing software for just about as long. My migraines have changed over the years and I've tried many different types of treatment; clinical drugs, herbal remedies, lifestyle changes, meditation. Some things have had greater success than others but nothing has really had a dramatic effect. It looks like I'm stuck with them.

A few years ago I started keeping a log of when the attacks happen which was recommended by a health professional. As the amount of attacks recorded grew I wondered if I could do something useful with this information. The result of that is Migraine.Report.

Migraine.Report

This website will not cure your migraines. It will however give you some awareness around when they occur and it will let you browse your attacks using insightful data visualisations. It gives you an easy way to log and search your attacks and works just as well on desktop, tablet or mobile. If you're already logging your migraines then it's easy to import them as either a spreadsheet (.xls, .xlsx, .csv) or from calendar software (Outlook, Google Calendar) as .ics files.

I have found it useful to log and view my migraines here and I hope you do too. If you do have any suggestions as to how I could improve Migraine.Report then I'd love to hear them. Thank you.
